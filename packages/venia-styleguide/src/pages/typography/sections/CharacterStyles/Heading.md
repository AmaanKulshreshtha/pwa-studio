### Heading

Use heading styles to establish hierarchy through typography. Venia provides eight heading sizes:

***

Size  | Type Scale Value
:--- | :---
XXXL | font-size-1200
XXL | font-size-1000
XL | font-size-800
L | font-size-600
M | font-size-400
S | font-size-300
XS | font-size-200
XXS | font-size-100
